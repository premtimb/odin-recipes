Pizza

Description

A classic Italian favorite, this pizza has a crispy thin crust topped with savory tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your choice of fresh toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers.

Perfect for a quick lunch or a fun family dinner!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni or other toppings
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your chosen toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
