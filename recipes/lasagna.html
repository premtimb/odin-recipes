Classic Italian Lasagna

This lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for a family dinner!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook beef and onion.
  3. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese. Bake for 40 minutes.
