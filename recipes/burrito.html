Mexican Burrito

A delicious Mexican-inspired burrito filled with seasoned meat, rice, beans, and fresh vegetables. Wrapped in a warm tortilla, itâ€™s perfect for a filling lunch or dinner on the go!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Cooked rice
  • Black beans or pinto beans
  • Grilled chicken, beef, or tofu
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Salsa and sour cream
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla in a pan or microwave.
  2. Layer rice, beans, and protein in the center of the tortilla.
  3. Add lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
  4. Fold the sides and roll tightly into a burrito.
  5. Serve warm with extra salsa or sour cream.
